About

Being mechanically and technically inclined with a creative streak, a never ending curiosity, an obsession with perfection and a love of the mountains and nature, my journey finally led me Portland to live in this beautiful Pacific Wonderland in Silicon Forest.

I've worked for billion dollar multi-national corporations such as PepsiCo/ Frito-Lay and Heidelberg Cement Group to small-scale family-run businesses in America and Japan, improving their performance, websites, web portals, services and products. I have a unique combination of multi-disciplined technical experience, technical writing, digital craftsmanship, multi-lingual ability, web development and design skills, all applied with stringent Quality Assurance principles. I am always learning, always improving my craft and follow the Japanese philosophy of Kaizen 改善.

Outside of programming and 3D printing, I enjoy exercise, building computers, Japanese things, the lush green woods and mountains of the Northwest, strong coffee, the Oregon Coast, driving on winding mountain roads and playing arcade games at Ground Kontrol in downtown Portland.
